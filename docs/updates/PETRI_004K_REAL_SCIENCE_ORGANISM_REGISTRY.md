# PETRI 004K - Real Science Organism Registry

004K installs a selectable organism registry as the source of truth for the right-side cards and dish rendering. The registry includes E. coli-like, Bacillus subtilis-like, Saccharomyces cerevisiae-like, and Acanthamoeba-like educational proxies. Cards can be selected/deselected, and only selected organisms render into the dish. Claim boundary: exploratory simulation only, not wet-lab evidence or species identification.
