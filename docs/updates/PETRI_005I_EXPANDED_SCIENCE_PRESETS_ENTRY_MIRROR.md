# PETRI 005I - Expanded Science Presets + Entry Mirror

## What 005I does
- expands the organism card registry with many more source-gated organism cards,
- expands the preset registry so each preset selects a different card set,
- adds intervention cards for antibiotic, antifungal, surface, light, and antibody-panel style educational presets,
- generates a human-readable preset card mirror under `reports/bio/`,
- mirrors that card summary into the `ORGANISM_GATE.ps1` entry point.

## Source grounding
This pack is grounded in curated educational resources:
- EcoCyc / BioCyc,
- Saccharomyces Genome Database,
- FDA Bad Bug Book,
- NCBI Medical Microbiology / NCBI context references.

## Claim boundary
These cards are educational proxies. They are not wet-lab calibration assets, clinical workflows, biosafety guidance, treatment guidance, or species-identification outputs.
