# Claim Boundary

Petri Dish Pro is an exploratory biological simulation platform.

Allowed claims:

- Educational demonstration of spatial population dynamics.
- Prototype artifact architecture for biological simulation workflows.
- Reproducible toy modeling of nutrient, toxin, motility, and interaction fields.
- UI demonstration of microscope-style density visualization.

Disallowed claims:

- Clinical diagnosis.
- Treatment recommendation.
- Real organism behavior proof.
- Wet-lab validation.
- Drug efficacy prediction.
- Epidemiological forecasting.
- Patient-level or public-health decision support.

Every run writes a validation receipt that restates this boundary.
