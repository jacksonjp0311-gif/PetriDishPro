
# Claim Boundary

Petri Dish Pro is an educational and research-oriented simulation sandbox.

It is not a diagnostic tool, clinical tool, wet-lab validation system, biosafety tool, or biological proof engine.

Every simulation run must be treated as a model artifact with parameters, assumptions, validation receipts, and explicit limits.


## PETRI 004K Claim Boundary
Organism registry cards are science-grounded proxies for educational simulation. They are not microscopy evidence, diagnostic outputs, species identification, treatment guidance, wet-lab proof, or biosafety evidence.


## PETRI 004O Claim Boundary
Preset, drug, and antibody cards are exploratory simulation controls and data-attachment slots. They are not clinical, diagnostic, susceptibility, treatment, wet-lab, or biosafety evidence.


## PETRI 004P Claim Boundary
Drug, antibody, organism, and metric cards are source-gated simulation cards. Without imported records containing source, record id, units, assay/method, date, and claim boundary, cards remain educational proxies only.
